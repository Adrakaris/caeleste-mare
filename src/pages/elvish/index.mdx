<Drop>V</Drop>_en'na_ \[文話\] is the language of the elves of _Halvanai_, also known as the Tianyuan Continent (天元大陸). It is spoken by elvenkind in East Aseron in the material plane, as well as the equivalent regions of the twilight realm, east of the _Kunnuu_ Mountains.
