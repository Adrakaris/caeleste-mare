# Song of Red Mountains
</ColourBand>
<CBox>

### English and Romanisation

In red mountains, in kingdom hall, / _Ith khansakh, ith hakaros khrae_,  
He walked proud and high, / _Aak dithins sirae_,  
Under the sun of autumn’s fall, / _Iisa asiir thera’on prin_,  
She danced through the sky. / _Khar su sara korins_.

In red mountains, in kingdom hall, / _Ith khansakh, ith hakaros khrae_,  
A wise sage resides, / _Vorn dranae xirin,_  
Under the ground in red crystal, / _Siia dir vathe ith polor khrae,_  
An ancient secret lies. / _Moras xinae xirin._

_\[chorus\]_  
Oh, my child, my warrior prince, / _Akh, ron xiyor, ron khalir khras,_  
Seek what lies beneath! / _Zisii tha, siia hirin!_  
An ancient secret, when unearthed, / _Moras xinae, as ankarin,_  
To you, great power bequeath. / _Tao zii, xian tanie malin._

From red mountains, from kingdom hall, / _Zon khansakh, zon hakaros khrae,_  
With a crest of flame, / _Mer lovii yirae,_  
Guided below by the Red God’s claw, / _Kaethins iisa me rekhaxana,_  
Down the depths he came. / _Iisa vath aak komins._

From red mountains, from kingdom hall, / _Zon khansakh, zon hakaros khrae,_  
Down to night’s domain, / _Tao kharik xarai,_  
And deep below in red crystal, / _A iisa vath ith polor khrae,_  
He saw his love again. / _Nadins aakon ixiir xin._

_\[chorus\]_
