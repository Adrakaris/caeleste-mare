A "ditto mark" ( `"` ) in an entry indicates it is identical to the one left of it. "nf." indicates not finished. Irregular verbs have conjugations shown; if a conjugation is missing from *rkaarik*, that indicates it is the same as its *rekharhakr* counterpart. Word categories are just a rough guide and many nouns especially can be used as adjectives. Words separated by spaces in the query do not need to be consecutive in the dictionary.
